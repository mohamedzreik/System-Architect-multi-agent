Inherently Social, Uniquely Discord

With the Embedded App SDK, developers have the power to reach millions of users by creating rich, immersive, and interactive experiences that live directly within Discord.Interactive Games

Create immersive multiplayer games to compete with friends, collaborate, or get the conversation started.Image and Video Remixing

Create generative AI apps that inspire the group chat with animation, image editing, and video remixing.Listen and Watch Parties

Transform music, podcasts, audiobooks, radio, and videos into shared experiences with friends.Game Integrations

Connect players with their favorite games. Leaderboards, stats, and shareable moments keep the fun going outside the game.The Totally Uncategorizable

Share cat facts. Water and grow a virtual tree with your friends. Show off your skills by catching and selling fish. Spice up the chat with Truth or Dare. Bring fun to the chat in unexpected ways.

Get Discovered

Right in Chat

Show up where people hang out. Friends can instantly see what's happening and play together, no install necessary.

In the Directory

Publish your app to the Directory to get discovered, set up shop, and promote your app right inside of Discord.

On Any Device

Apps are instantly available on every device for people to find, use, and keep coming back to.

Grow your
business

A better path to product-market fit

From idea to launch, it all lives on Discord. Grow a tight-knit community of early adopters who provide feedback and champion what you build. Reach them and their friends with every new feature and update.

Hassle-free payments

Take advantage of Discord's integrated payment systems that users know, trust, and already use. Focus on innovation rather than financial logistics.
