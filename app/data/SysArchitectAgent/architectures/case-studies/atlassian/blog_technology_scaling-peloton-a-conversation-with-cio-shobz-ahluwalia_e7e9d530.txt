Scaling Peloton: a conversation with CIO Shobz Ahluwalia

Find out how Peloton was able to refine the online experience for both customers and employees.

A highlight of our annual conference,
Team 21
, was Atlassian Co-Founder and Co-CEO Scott Farquhar's chat with Shobz Ahluwalia, the CIO of Peloton.

In this interview, Scott and Shobz discuss how Peloton was able to scale to millions of users to meet the surging demand during the COVID-19 pandemic, support its new employees onboarding remotely, and more.
